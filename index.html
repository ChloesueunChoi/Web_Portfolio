Chloe's paintings

I am really into Minhwa,Traditional Korean Folk Art Painting 🖼️

Meaning A symbol of the knightly spirit and a liberated mind 🌿
Looking at "파초" (Parcho), it continually pushes out new leaves. Its ability to sprout fresh shoots every spring has become a symbol of strong vitality and unwavering loyalty.
Parcho represents the spirit of continuous self-improvement and unyielding determination, often expressed as "자강불식自强不息". Because of this, people held Parcho in high regard and affection.

Meaning Flower heralding spring, magnolia 🌸
Magnolias, which burst their buds to greet spring, are loved by people and frequently appear in poetry and paintings.
Resembling lotus flowers attached to trees, they are called '목련木蓮' (magnolia) and are often depicted in folk paintings to resemble lotus flowers.
Sometimes blooming in white and other times in red, these magnolias, often seen alongside rocks, are symbols of longevity.
